About my favorite author

The most favorite author of mine

I've read many books all this time. But my all-time-favorite book of mine is the "Diary of the Wimpy Kid" series.
The series' author is Jeff Kinney. Jeff Kinney is a #1 New York
Times bestselling author and a six-time Nickelodeon Kids' Choice
Award winner for Favorite Book. Jeff has been named one of Time
magazine's 100 Most Influential People in the World. He is also
the creator of Poptropica, which was named one of Time magazine's
50 Best Websites. He spent his childhood in the Washington, D.C., area
and moved to New England in 1995. Jeff lives with his wife and
two sons in Massachusetts, where they own a bookstore, An Unlikely Story.

My favorite books of his is "The Diary of a Wimpy Kid Getaway", "The Diary of a Wimpy Kid Old School",
"The Diary of a Wimpy Kid Double Down", and "The Diary of a Wimpy Kid Wrecking Ball".
The main character is Greg Heffley. Greg's family is a CRAZY family. His mother Susan Heffley tries new things and she wants her son
Greg to use his creativity to do new things and always saying what are the things she
did in her childhood. And his father Frank Heffley is a guy who thinks buying things
which are too expensive is useless. And he has a big brother whose name is Rodrick Heffley
who often play dirty pranks and tell lies to Greg. And poor Greg just believe on him.
And his friend Rowley is a doofus.
